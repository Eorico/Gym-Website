Leg Workout

Strengthen and sculpt your legs with these effective exercises

Top Leg Exercises

Squats

Squats are one of the most effective exercises for building overall leg strength. They target the quads, hamstrings, glutes, and core.
Lunges

Lunges are a great unilateral exercise that targets the quads, hamstrings, and glutes, improving balance and stability.
Leg Press

The leg press is a machine-based exercise that targets the quads, hamstrings, and glutes, allowing you to lift heavy weights safely.
Deadlifts

Deadlifts target the hamstrings, glutes, and lower back. It's a compound movement that strengthens the posterior chain.
Bulgarian Split Squats

Bulgarian split squats are an advanced single-leg exercise that focuses on the quads, hamstrings, and glutes. They also improve balance and stability.
Calf Raises

Calf raises target the calves and help improve lower leg strength and stability, making them an essential part of any leg workout.

legs Workout Details

Beginner Workout:

3 sets of 12 repetitions
NOTE: Focus on learning proper form and control. Use lighter weights to build endurance and technique.

Intermediate Workout:

4 sets of 8-10 repetitions
NOTE: Increase weight slightly to challenge strength.
Maintain proper form and gradually reduce rest times between sets.

Veteran Workout:

5 sets of 5-6 repetitions
NOTE: Use heavier weights to focus on maximum strength.
Increase intensity with shorter rest intervals.
Include techniques like pause reps (pausing at the bottom of the lift) for greater time under tension.
