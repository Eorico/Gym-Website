Full-Body Workout

Get stronger and fitter with these all-around full body exercises

Top Full-Body Exercises

Burpees

Burpees are a full-body exercise that targets the chest, arms, legs, and core. It also improves cardiovascular endurance.
Push-Ups

Push-ups are a classic upper body exercise that primarily targets the chest, shoulders, and triceps. They also engage the core.
Jump Squats

Jump squats are a plyometric exercise that works the quads, hamstrings, glutes, and calves, while also increasing explosive power.
Mountain Climbers

Mountain climbers are a high-intensity exercise that targets the core, legs, and arms while also boosting cardiovascular endurance.
Deadlifts

Deadlifts work the posterior chain, engaging the hamstrings, glutes, lower back, and core. It's a highly effective compound movement.
Kettlebell Swings

Kettlebell swings are a dynamic movement that works the hips, glutes, quads, hamstrings, and core, while also improving power and endurance.

Full-Body Workout Details

Beginner Workout:

3 sets of 12 repetitions
NOTE: Focus on learning proper form and control. Use lighter weights to build endurance and technique.

Intermediate Workout:

4 sets of 8-10 repetitions
NOTE: Increase weight slightly to challenge strength.
Maintain proper form and gradually reduce rest times between sets.

Veteran Workout:

5 sets of 5-6 repetitions
NOTE: Use heavier weights to focus on maximum strength.
Increase intensity with shorter rest intervals.
Include techniques like pause reps (pausing at the bottom of the lift) for greater time under tension.
