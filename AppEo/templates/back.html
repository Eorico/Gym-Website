Back Workout

Strengthen and define your back with these top exercises

Top Back Exercises

Deadlifts

Deadlifts are a compound movement that targets the lower back, glutes, and hamstrings. They are one of the most effective strength-building exercises.
Lat Pulldowns

Lat pulldowns are great for targeting the latissimus dorsi muscles. They help develop width and strength in the upper back.
Barbell Rows

Barbell rows are a great compound exercise that targets the upper back and rear delts, helping build overall back strength.
T-Bar Rows

T-Bar rows target the middle back and are great for adding thickness to the back. Use a wide grip to engage more of the rear delts.
Pull-ups

Pull-ups are a bodyweight exercise that primarily works the lats and the biceps. They are excellent for building overall back strength and size.
Seated Rows

Seated rows are a great exercise to focus on the mid-back, helping develop thickness and strength in the upper back.

Back Workout Details

Beginner Workout:

3 sets of 12 repetitions
NOTE: Focus on learning proper form and control. Use lighter weights to build endurance and technique.

Intermediate Workout:

4 sets of 8-10 repetitions
NOTE: Increase weight slightly to challenge strength.
Maintain proper form and gradually reduce rest times between sets.

Veteran Workout:

5 sets of 5-6 repetitions
NOTE: Use heavier weights to focus on maximum strength.
Increase intensity with shorter rest intervals.
Include techniques like pause reps (pausing at the bottom of the lift) for greater time under tension.
