Abs Workout

Strengthen and define your core with these top ab exercises

Top Abs Exercises

Crunches

Crunches are a simple yet effective exercise that targets the upper abdominals. Focus on controlled movements to maximize engagement.
Leg Raises

Leg raises primarily target the lower abdominals and help improve overall core strength. Keep your movements controlled to engage your core fully.
Planks

Planks are an isometric exercise that engages your entire core. Hold the position for as long as possible to build endurance and strength.
Bicycle Crunches

Bicycle crunches are great for engaging the obliques and upper abs. Perform slow, controlled movements to target the core muscles effectively.
Russian Twists

Russian twists are great for building strength in the obliques and improving rotational core stability. Use a weight for added resistance.
Mountain Climbers

Mountain climbers are a full-body exercise that also targets the core, improving cardiovascular endurance while engaging the abs.

Arms Workout Details

Beginner Workout:

3 sets of 12 repetitions
NOTE: Focus on learning proper form and control. Use lighter weights to build endurance and technique.

Intermediate Workout:

4 sets of 8-10 repetitions
NOTE: Increase weight slightly to challenge strength.
Maintain proper form and gradually reduce rest times between sets.

Veteran Workout:

5 sets of 5-6 repetitions
NOTE: Use heavier weights to focus on maximum strength.
Increase intensity with shorter rest intervals.
Include techniques like pause reps (pausing at the bottom of the lift) for greater time under tension.
